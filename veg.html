Ratatouille

As despised by French schoolchildren (mine anyway) as it is loved by cartoon rats, ratatouille is the undisputed king of French vegetarian food. And it’s vegan, too. Prepared badly, it can be a watery, sloppy, gloop of a dish with spongy textures and bitter flavours. But done well – well that’s something else altogether: tender, aromatic vegetables, scented with herbs and gushing with juice.
